Our Heritage

Rooted in the Art of
Bagru Block Printing

Bagru is a small town on the outskirts of Jaipur, Rajasthan — and the birthplace of one of India's most celebrated craft traditions. For centuries, artisans here have passed down the art of hand block-printing, using hand-carved wooden blocks, natural plant-based dyes, and pure cotton fabrics.

At Bagru Vastrakala, we work directly with these artisan families to bring their craft to customers across India — preserving livelihoods, culture, and the extraordinary beauty of handmade textiles.
